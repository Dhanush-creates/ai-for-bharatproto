# Future Scope – Connected Accounts Personalization

Aurora AI is designed to evolve into a platform-aware personalization system where users can connect Instagram and LinkedIn accounts separately. By analyzing previous post history, content patterns, tone, and platform behavior, the system can generate more personalized, persona-aligned content instead of generic outputs. This enables history-aware content repurposing, persona-based generation, platform-style continuation, and new generation modes tailored to the user’s publishing identity and audience style.

## History-Aware Content Repurposing

Once connected, Aurora AI will fetch and analyze previous post history for each user. By understanding historical posting styles, tone nuances, and engagement metrics, Aurora AI will offer **Persona-Based Content Repurposing**. This history-aware analysis unlocks advanced generation modes, such as automatically suggesting engagement-aware rewrites and audience-aligned content tailored directly to the creator's precise digital footprint.

## Persona-Based Generation

Users will connect Instagram and LinkedIn to allow the AI to analyze past publishing semantics and engagement patterns, unlocking *Persona-Based Content Repurposing* tailored precisely to the user's historical voice.
